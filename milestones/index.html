Milestones

This document is intended to help Chairs and Team Contacts in planning their deadlines for specification milestones.

In theory, it takes a minimum of around 174 days to move a specification to W3C Recommendation, without ongoing exclusion opportunities. On the first day (Day 1), you request the transition to First Public Working Draft and so on until the publication of the W3C Recommendation on the 174th day.

The computation takes into account the W3C Process, the W3C Patent Policy, and the transition requirements.
